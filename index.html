Top 5 Killstreaks

5. Predator Missle

Predator Missle is a 5 point killstreak which lets you control a rocket coming straight down onto the map. If used correctly and with a little luck, this can help kill multiple players at once; which can lead to your other killstreaks. This can also take out other people's killstreaks in the sky or ground. It's versality and low amount of kills to get, makes this a top 5 killstreak in MW3

4. AC-130

AC-130 is a 12 point killstreak which lets you control a gunship's array of 3 missle types. A classic killstreak from MW2, this does require some some sort of skill because of the limited camera angles; however if used correctly, you can get a good amount of kills using this. It's variety of missles makes this a nightmare for the opposing team, and allows your squad to get an advantage in whatever gamemode.. which makes this a top 5 killstreak in MW3.

3. Pave Low

Pave Low is a 12 point killstreak which summons a heavily armored assault helicopter that attacks enemies. The Pave Low is harder to destroy and packs a bigger punch than the regular Attack Helicopter. If used in a open map with not that much buildings, this can cause alot of damage to the opposition.. as well as it helping you get your higher killstreaks.

2. Reaper

Reaper is a 9 point killstreak which allows you to control a gunship with approximately 9 guided missles ready to be unleashed on the enemy. It has a smooth, slow and accurate gun fire compare to the AC-130, however, both camera angles are the same. If used correctly, can rack up kills in a hurry.

1. Osprey Gunner

Osprey Gunner is a 17 point killstreak which allows you to control a gunship with a machine gun, and drops 4 care packages for your squad. Hands down the best killstreak in this game because of how easy it is to get kills, as well as it giving your squad a chance to get useful killstreaks through the care package.
